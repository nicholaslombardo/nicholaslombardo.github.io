Animated Figures

These are animated figures from the Titan Through Time V poster.

Animated climatology of HCN abundance. Circles represent locations of CIRS observations, colored by abundance.

Abundance at 'future' dates (post 2017) are derived from the corresponding measurements from the opposte hemisphere offset by 180 Ls.

Climatologies also exist for ethane, ethene, ethyne, propyne, and cyanoacetylene.

Animated climatology of aerosol extinction coefficient (beta). There is a typo in the label - the units are the base-10 log of beta.
